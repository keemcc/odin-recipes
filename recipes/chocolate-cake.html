Chocolate Cake

Description

A refreshing and creamy Indian yogurt-based drink blended with sweet mangoes, perfect for hot summer days. This traditional beverage combines the richness of yogurt with the tropical sweetness of ripe mangoes, creating a smooth and satisfying treat that's both nutritious and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cold milk
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Pinch of salt
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash, peel, and chop the ripe mangoes into small pieces.
  2. Add the chopped mangoes to a blender along with the yogurt, milk, and sugar.
  3. Add the ground cardamom and a pinch of salt to enhance the flavors.
  4. Add ice cubes to the blender for a chilled drink.
  5. Blend all ingredients on high speed for 1-2 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  6. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more sugar if needed.
  7. Pour the Chocolate Cake into tall glasses.
  8. Garnish with fresh mint leaves or a sprinkle of cardamom if desired.
  9. Serve immediately while cold and enjoy!
